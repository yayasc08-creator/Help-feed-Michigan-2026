Get Help with Food Assistance

🍽️ SNAP (Food Stamps)

SNAP helps individuals and families buy groceries using a Bridge Card in Michigan.

  • Apply online through MI Bridges
  • Call: (855) 275-6424
  • Visit a local MDHHS office for help

🍼 WIC Program

WIC provides food, nutrition education, and support for pregnant women, new mothers, and children under 5.

  • Call your local WIC office
  • Schedule an appointment
  • Bring ID and proof of income
